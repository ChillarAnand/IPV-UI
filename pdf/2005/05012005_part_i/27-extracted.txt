(57) Abstract :  [Subject] Disclosed here is a method for supporting such lights 
as a tail light, etc. surely and firmly with use of a rear fork by a simplified 
structure in a motorcycle in which the rear fork that journals the rear wheel is 
connected to a rear portion of the vehicle body frame so as to be oscillated 
vertically and the rear fork is suspended to the vehicle body frame through a 
rear cushion. 
[Solving Means] The base of the supporting stay ST is fastened to a rear 
portion of the rear fork FR and the supporting stay ST is extended upward 
over the rear fender 29 that covers the rear wheel WR and such lights 40 as the 
tail light 38, etc. are fastened to the top portion 47 of the supporting stay ST.
