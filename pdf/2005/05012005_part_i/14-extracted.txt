Abstract => A process for isolation of estrogen and corticosteroids mixtures form cow urine 
comprising of extraction of Cow urine with non-polar organic solvents is extraction is repeated three 
times and organic layer is evaporated after addition of 1-2 ml of oil or alcohol at room temperature in 
order to obtain pure estrogen and cortisosteroids, and the ratio of urine to non-polar solvent is 4:2.
