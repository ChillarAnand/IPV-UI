Title => AIR PUMP ACTUATING APPARATUS 
 

International Classification => (71) 
Abstract => In an air pump actuating apparatus for 
actuating an air pump with an engine, to reduce the 
number of parts thereof and also reduce the size of an 
overall engine combine therewith , to install the air 
pump actuating apparatus easily on a vehicle or the like, 
and to increase the freedom with to set the speed 
changing ration of an air-pump thereof. [solving means] 
A 4-cycle engine has a camshaft 41 serving a portion of 
a valve operating device 38 and disposed in a cylinder 
block 13. an air pump 61 to which power from the 
camshaft 41 is transmitted has a pump case 63 
integrally formed with the cylinder block 13.
