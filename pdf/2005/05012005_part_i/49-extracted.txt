Title => WINDSHIELD APPARATUS FOR MOTORCYCLES 
 

International Classification => (71) 
Abstract => A process for the recovery of lignin from black liquor comprising in the steps of: 
a).  mixing the black liquor with an acid so as to produce a flocculated slurry of precipitated lignin and black 
liquor. 
b).  introducing the flocculated slurry into a filter bed containing a plurality of sand and gravel beds to cause 
a separation of the lignin. 
c).  the filtrate from the bed being subjected to primary clarification with mill effluent.
