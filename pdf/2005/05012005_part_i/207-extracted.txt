Title => METHOD OF INTRODUCING MELAMINE OFF-GASES INTO A UREA  
                                       PLANT 
 

International Classification => C 07 C 273/12 (71) 
Abstract => The invention relates to a method for producing urea.  According to said method, the off-gases which 
originate from a melamine plant and essentially consist of NH3 and CO2 are introduced into the high-
pressure zone of the urea plant by means of ejectors.
