Date of filing of application number => 16/02/2001
Title => FLAME RESISTANT FABRICS 
 

International Classification => D 03 D 15/12 (71) 
Abstract => The present invention provides a woven flame resistant fabric comprising dissimilar warp and fill 
yarns, the warp yarns comprise staple or filament fibers and have a Limiting Oxygen Index of at least 
27, and the fill yarns comprise natural fibers and wherein the ratio of warp to fill yarn ends in the 
fabric is at least 1.0.
