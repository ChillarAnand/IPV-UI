Title => FABRIC SLEEVE FOR A RUBBER TUBE 
 

International Classification => D 04 C 1/02  (71) 
Abstract => A protective fabric sleeve (1) for a rubber tube 
comprises heat-recoverable interlaced strands 
forming an elastically deformable tubular 
structure, the strands comprising at least one 
monofilament yarn (2) and at least one 
multifilament yarn (3). Use especially for a 
coupling in a fluid circuits of a motor vehicle.
