Title => ADJUSTABLE LENGTH VASCULAR OCCLUSION DEVICE 
 

International Classification => A 61 B 17/12 (71) 
Abstract => The invention concerns electrolytically detachable biocompatible metal wire turns.  The invention 
also concerns devices comprising such turns and a conductor guide compatible in shape and size 
with their axial translation displacement in a catheter.  Said turns and devices are designed for setting 
electrolytically detachable vascular occlusion turns with adjustable length.
