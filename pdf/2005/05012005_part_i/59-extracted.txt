Title => VEHICLE BODY COVER AND SEAT LOCK MOUNTING STRUCTURE FOR A SCOOTER-TYPE VEHICLE. 
       

International Classification => (71) 
Abstract => To facilitate the formation of a cylinder head requiring the 
working of plural holes different in a working shaft for intake and exhaust valves 
and a plug tube and to provide a head hanger that contributes to the stable support 
of a engine. 
 
A cylinder block 3, a cylinder head 4 and a cylinder head cover 5 are stacked in 
the order and a valve train of a SOHC type provided with an intake valve 16 and 
an exhaust valve 17 is provided to the cylinder head 4.  A head cover joint line 
L2 parallel with the joint of the cylinder head 4 and the cylinder head cover 5 is 
inclined forward and downward for a cylinder joint line L1 parallel with the joint 
of the cylinder block 3 and the cylinder head 4, and the axis J2 of the exhaust 
valve 17 and the axis J3 of a plug tube 50 are perpendicular to the head cover 
joint line L2.  A head hanger 19 is integrated at the uppermost end of the back of 
the cylinder head 4 over an intake port 12.
