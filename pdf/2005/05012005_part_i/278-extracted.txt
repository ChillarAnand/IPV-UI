Date of filing of application number => 18/07/2001
Title => METHOD TO AID SMOKING CESSATION 
 

International Classification => A 61 K 31/22 (71) 
Abstract => This invention relates to a method to aid smoking cessation by treating a patient who wishes to stop 
smoking with an effective amount of a compound, in particular a phenylcyclobutylbutylamine, in 
order to aid the patient in overcoming the craving following smoking cessation.  The method relates 
to preventing weight gain in a patient who ceases to smoke and optionally is using a nicotine patch or 
other agent or program to aid in ceasing to smoke.
