Application number => 727/MUM/2003
Date of filing of application number => 21/07/2003
Title => A METHOD OF STORING RAIN WATER AND A STORAGE SYSTEM  
                                        FOR THE SAME. 
       

International Classification => (71) 
Abstract => A method of storing rain water comprising construction of trapezoidal reservoir or storage tank and a plurality of water collection cum 
settlement tanks all below ground level by excavating soil such that the top of the tank kept at highest level available in the selected area and the 
collection cum settlement tanks are kept lowest level in the area, the reservoir and collection tanks being connected by cast iron piping and pumping of 
water from collection tanks to reservoir is done by means of electrical motor-pump set.
