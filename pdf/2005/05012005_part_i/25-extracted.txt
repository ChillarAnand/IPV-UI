Application number => 731/MUM/2003
Date of filing of application number => 21/07/2003
Title => A KEYPAD DEPLOYED TO INPUT INDIC SCRIPTS ON  
                                        HAND-HELD DEVICES. 
                                         
       

International Classification => (71) 
Abstract => A numerical keypad for character entries in Indic scripts consisting of 12 to 15 keys: consisting of eight keys having two layers one on 
which the basic consonants capable of aspiration or extension are distributed and a second layer on which the vowels are distributed; a toggle key for 
switching layers; an extender key for converting the selected basic consonant or vowel to its extended form; a truncator key for converting the consonant 
into its truncated form either as a halant or as a Nukta and an independent key for character corresponding to the phonetic sound ‘H’.
