Application number => 749/MUM/2003
Date of filing of application number => 29/07/2003
Title => A PHOTOGRAPHIC PROCESSING SYSTEM. 
       

International Classification => (71) 
Abstract => A two twin track edges razor comprising six components, a head, an upper smaller blade, a separator, a bigger standard blade, a base and 
a handle wherein the upper smaller blade, the separator, the lower standard blade and the base each having central circular cutout and symmetrical side 
cutouts corresponding to centrally located threaded projection and symmetrically located side projections in the head and wherein the standard lower 
blade being approximately 2.00 mm larger in width than the upper smaller blade adapted to protrude approximately 1.00 mm on both sides of the upper 
smaller blade when assembled and wherein said separator being diecut in predetermined shape from a uniform flat 1.00 mm thick plastic or rubber sheet 
with concave edges adapted to collect “goo” formed by the upper smaller blade and wherein parallel edges of the head and the base being curved to 
predetermined shape adapted to support the blades at an angle which give comfortable shave and wherein said razor being assembled on the threaded 
projection in the head by inserting the upper smaller blade then the separator, the lower standard blade and the base and then being tight fitted by 
screwing the internal threads of the handle on the said threaded projection.
