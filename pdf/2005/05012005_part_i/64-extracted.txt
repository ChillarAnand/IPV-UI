Title => A STORAGE WATER HEATER WITH A DIGITALLY PROGRAMMABLE ON/OFF TIMER. 
       

International Classification => (71) 
Abstract => This invention relates to a storage water heater with a digitally 
programmable on/off timer comprising: (i) an outer housing, (ii) an inner housing 
disposed within said outer housing, (iii) an insulation disposed between said 
inner and outer housing, (iv) an inlet for introduction of water to be heated, (v) an 
outlet for discharge of heated water, (vi) a heating element disposed within said 
inner housing and adapted to be connected to a power source, (vii) characterized 
in that programmable digital timer for automatically connecting or disconnecting 
the heating element from the power source, at the set time, (viii) a real time clock 
provided in the digital timer, (ix) a back lit timer display and clock display, and 
(x) a back up rechargeable battery.
