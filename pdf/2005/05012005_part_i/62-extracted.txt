Application number => 818/MUM/2003
Date of filing of application number => 18/08/2003
Title => STABLE PHARMACEUTICAL COMPOSITION OF RABEPRAZOLE. 
       

International Classification => (71) 
Abstract => A process for preparation of pharmaceutical composition of rebeprazole and its salts thereof, with a shelf life of at least 6 months, comprising 
steps of  
a. Sulubilization of rabeprazole in a solvent 
b. Dissolution of lactose or galactose or trehalose or their combination with or without other excipients under stirring 
c. Adjusting pH to 8.0-11.0 
d. Optionally removing any particulates from the solution 
e. Lyophilization of the solution in an appropriate container.
