Title => METHOD FOR PRODUCING BISPHENOL-A. 
 

International Classification => C 07 C 39/16 (71) 
Abstract => The invention relates to a method for producing light-colored bisphenol-A having residual 
contents of oxygen and phenol from mixed crystals of bisphenol-A and phenol.  The bisphenol-A is 
produced by melting the crystals, inerting them with nitrogen and by feeding them into a distillation system.
