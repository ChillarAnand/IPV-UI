(57) Abstract : 
The present invention comprises identification of active compound of formula I obtained from C. auriculata leaves for the 
development of new herbal drug having better therapeutic potential for treatment  of  
diabetes  and  its  complications.  The  present  invention  also  demonstrates antihyperglycemic, hypolipidemic, antioxidant and 
antiatherosclerotic activity of the compound. The present invention also provides formulations comprising said active compound of 
Formula I. The present invention also relates to method for obtaining active compound of Formula I.
